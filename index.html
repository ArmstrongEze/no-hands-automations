Before You Ask

Common questions

—Will this work with the EMR we already use?

In most cases, yes. I build around what you currently use rather than asking you to switch systems — the goal is one less thing for your team to learn, not one more.

—Is our patient data safe?

Patient data is encrypted at rest, and every action that touches a record is logged. The system is built with HIPAA-conscious handling from day one, not added after the fact.

—How long does a build like this take?

It depends on scope, but most single-workflow builds (booking, or reminders, or response handling) take one to two weeks from our first call to a live, tested system.

—What happens if something breaks after launch?

I monitor what I build. Ongoing support is part of the offer, not a separate emergency call — if a workflow misfires, I fix it.

—Does this replace our front desk staff?

No — it removes the repetitive parts of their job (re-typing bookings, chasing confirmations by phone) so they can spend their time actually helping patients in front of them.
